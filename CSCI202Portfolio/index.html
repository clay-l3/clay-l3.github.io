Email Exchange with someone from 1995

A email responding to someone who wrote to me about the future from 1995:

Student: "Hello, I'm from the year 1995 and I was on a website that said it would send emails to the future. So I am writing this to you to ask what it's like in the future. Are there flying cars yet? What is the internet like?" Me: "Hello I received your email! I am indeed from the future! The year 2025! Sadly, no flying cars but we do have cars that are fully electric! In terms of the Internet, the Internet is a MASSIVE part of our daily lives. We can around phones in our pockets that gives access to the internet 24/7! This allows us to send messages to friends, relatives, strangers, etc. Assist us with our jobs/school. It has also shaped the way our culture changes and how we interact with others everyday. I could go into so much detail but I would be here all day so just know, It's going to be a big part of life in the future!"
